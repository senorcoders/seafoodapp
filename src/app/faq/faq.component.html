Marketplace is an online B2B marketplace that gives buyers the tools to order all their food in one place, direct from any local & international seller.

This means that all food buyers and sellers can register to use our online marketplace - for free

  • Our online marketplace aims to be your one-stop-shop for all your food needs, we want to make your life easier by giving you the tools to order all your products in one place.
  • For the first time you have the ability to choose where your food is coming from through our online catalogue where you can search for products based on your own choice of filters i.e. origin, treatment, capture methods and many more.
  • Marketplace gives you the tools to order direct from source which helps save on warehousing costs, ensure maximum freshness of products and guarantee 100% traceability.

No warehousing, no storage.
Your order will be shipped from the producer e.g. food producers, Fishermen, food farms, direct to your establishment - from Sea to B.

By ordering direct from source you are guaranteed 100% traceability. Your product is tracked from the moment it is caught until it is delivered to you.

Marketplace is 100% free to sign up, all it costs is a few minutes of your time to register.
